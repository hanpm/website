Hello. We are Women in Computing.

A student organization that sets out to promote mentorship, community, and growth for aspiring women in coding. I WANNA BE DRUNK BUT ;-;

Our Mission

A new wave of engineers in computer science aims to provide a powerful community to uplift and improve ongoing growth of women in tech. We are here to provide a platform to cultivate leadership, confidence and excellence in aspiring women in STEM. Together, we provide a community that hones ethical leadership, a sisterhood that uplifts women and fosters self-worth, and a sense of belonging for future students of CSULB to come.
